About the Site

  • Practice locating major cities on an interactive globe.
  • You can only make three attempts per city, and it will only count as correct if you get it on your first try. After that the correct location will be revealed.
  • World: Easy – 10 cities, Hard – 30 cities
  • Small Nations: All countries with 1-3 important cities
  • Individual Countries: Easy – 3 cities, Hard – 10 cities
  • Custom: Not implemented yet – User can type amount of cities to practice. Default: All important major cities (the ones that appear bold on Google Maps or are important for Geoguessr)
