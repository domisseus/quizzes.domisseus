1. Passive with Ser
Formula: Object + Ser + Participle + (por + Agent)
*Ser is conjugated in the active tense.
*Participle agrees in gender/number with Object.
TenseExample
PresenteEl libro es escrito por Juan.
(The book is written by Juan.)
PretéritoLos libros fueron escritos por Juan.
(The books were written by Juan.)
ImperfectoEl libro era leído por los niños.
(The book was read by the children.)
FuturoEl libro será escrito por Juan.
(The book will be written by Juan.)
CondicionalEl libro sería escrito por Juan.
(The book would be written by Juan.)
Present PerfectEl libro ha sido escrito por Juan.
(The book has been written by Juan.)
Past PerfectEl libro había sido escrito por Juan.
(The book had been written by Juan.)
Future PerfectEl libro habrá sido escrito por Juan.
(The book will have been written by Juan.)
Cond. PerfectEl libro habría sido escrito por Juan.
(The book would have been written by Juan.)
2. Passive Reflexive
Usage: More common in spoken Spanish.
Formula: Se + Verb (3rd person) + Object
SpanishEnglish
Se vende la casa.The house is sold.
Se venden libros aquí.Books are sold here.
Se alquilan apartamentos.Apartments are rented.
3. Impersonal Passive
Usage: Agent is irrelevant or general.
Formula: Se + Verb (3rd singular)
SpanishEnglish
Se dice que lloverá.It is said that it will rain.
Se cree que es verdad.It is believed to be true.
4. Causative (Hacer)
Definition: To make or have someone do something.
Common: Hacer + Infinitive
SpanishEnglish
Hice reparar el coche.I had the car repaired.
Te haré saber la respuesta.I'll let you know the answer.
Me hice cortar el pelo.I had my hair cut.
Se hizo construir una casa.He had a house built.
5. Other Causative Verbs
VerbExample / Meaning
Mandar / Enviar
(Order/Send)		Le mandé limpiar el garaje.
(I ordered him to clean the garage.)
Dejar
(Let/Allow)		Me dejó usar su coche.
(He let me use his car.)
Obligar a
(Force)		Lo obligaron a firmar.
(They forced him to sign.)
6. Prepositions (A vs Por)
PrepositionUsage / Example
AReceiver of the order:
La profesora hizo estudiar a Juan.
(The teacher made Juan study.)
PorAgent (By whom):
Hice pintar la casa por un profesional.
(I had the house painted by a professional.)
