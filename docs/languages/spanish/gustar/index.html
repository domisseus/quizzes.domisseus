1. How it Works
Structure: The subject is what is liked. The person who likes it is the indirect object.

Conjugation:
  • Singular (gusta): If the thing liked is singular.
  • Plural (gustan): If the thing liked is plural.
SpanishEnglish
Me gusta el libro.I like the book.
Me gustan los libros.I like the books.
Te gustan los perros.You like dogs.
Nos gusta bailar.We like to dance.
Les gustan las películas.They like movies.
No me gusta el café.I don't like coffee.
No les gustan las verduras.They don't like vegetables.

SentenceMeaning

Solo nos quedan dos semanas.We only have two weeks left.
